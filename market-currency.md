Endpoint: `https://(www|beta).avabur.com/api/market/currency`

Lists up to 10 market entries for each of games currencies: 
`Crystals`, `Platinum`, `Food`, `Wood`, `Stone`, `Iron`, `Crafting Materials` and `Gem Fragments`

**In case a currency would not have any listing on the market, it wil NOT be present in the response**
